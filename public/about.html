A Place of Compassion & Dignity in Oneida

At All Fatih Funeral Home, located at 130 Lenox Ave, Oneida, New York, we understand the weight of saying goodbye. Our home was established to serve as a sanctuary for families during their most tender moments, offering a steadfast hand and a gentle heart. We are not just a funeral service provider; we are your neighbors, dedicated to honoring the lives and legacies of those who have shaped our community.

Our dedicated funeral team brings together decades of experience in compassionate care, ensuring that every service is a true reflection of the individual. With a legacy spanning over 27,980 obituaries, we have had the profound privilege of walking alongside countless families. We believe that every farewell should be as unique as the life it celebrates, which is why we offer a comprehensive range of services and memorial products, from elegant caskets and candles to fresh flowers and personalized memorial cards.

We are committed to providing a warm, inviting space where memories are cherished and healing begins. Whether you are seeking immediate guidance or planning ahead, All Fatih Funeral Home is here to support you with integrity, reverence, and unwavering compassion.
